Who Are We

Founded in 2006 with just 29 students, Siret Secondary School has grown into a leading educational institution in Kipkelion, now serving over 600 students. We are committed to academic excellence and the holistic development of our students, providing a nurturing and supportive environment that helps each student reach their full potential. At Siret Secondary School, we uphold the core values of discipline, integrity, and teamwork. These values are central to our educational philosophy, resulting in well-rounded graduates who excel in higher education and contribute meaningfully to society. Our dedicated faculty members are passionate educators who not only impart knowledge but also inspire a love for lifelong learning. Beyond academics, we offer a dynamic co-curricular program that fosters creativity, leadership, and strong moral character. Students have the opportunity to participate in a variety of activities, including sports, clubs, and community service projects, all of which are essential to their personal growth and development.

Admission

Being a public school, the students are allocated to the school by the Ministry of Education.

  • Prospective students must participate in the national examination process.
  • The Ministry assigns school placements based on performance, preferences, and available slots.
  • Parents and guardians should ensure that their children meet all necessary academic and administrative requirements set by the Ministry.

While academic performance is a key factor in the selection process, we also consider participation in co-curricular activities, leadership qualities, and community service involvement.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to some of the common questions about Siret Secondary School.

Where is Siret Secondary School located?

Siret Secondary School is located along the C35 road in Kipkelion, Kericho County, Kenya.

What are the contact details for Siret Secondary School?

You can reach Siret Secondary School via the following contact details:

  • Phone: +254 700 000 000
  • Email: info@Siret Secondary Schoolsecondary.ac.ke
  • Postal Address: P.O. Box 10, Kipkelion, Kericho, Kenya

What programs and courses does Siret Secondary School offer?

Siret Secondary School offers a comprehensive curriculum in line with the Kenyan education system, including subjects in sciences, humanities, and technical fields. We also offer co-curricular activities such as sports, music, and drama.

How can I apply for admission to Siret Secondary School?

To apply for admission, please visit our school office to pick up an application form or download it from our website. Completed forms should be submitted along with the required documents. For more details, contact our admissions office.

What are the school fees and payment options?

School fees vary depending on the class level. We offer flexible payment options including bank transfers and mobile money. Detailed fee structures are provided upon admission, or you can contact our accounts office for more information.

What facilities does Siret Secondary School have?

Our school is equipped with modern facilities including science laboratories, a computer lab, a library and sports fields.
